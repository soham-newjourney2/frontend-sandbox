Our Global Campus

The IEM-UEM Group spans across Kolkata and Jaipur, offering world-class education in Engineering, Management, Science, and Commerce. The IEM campus in Salt Lake is renowned for discipline, modern facilities, and top placements, while the UEM campus in New Town, Kolkata provides a larger green campus with global collaborations and vibrant student culture. Expanding further, the UEM Jaipur campus offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, innovation labs, and a research-driven environment in a serene setting. Together, these campuses create a strong academic network with industry links, global exposure, and holistic student development.

About Us

We are a team of first-year students from IEM, Salt Lake, Kolkata, driven by a growing passion for software development. Our goal is to design websites and interfaces that serve social and community-focused purposes—tools that make a meaningful impact. We actively seek out opportunities to apply our skills in real-world projects, using each challenge as a chance to learn, experiment, and grow.
